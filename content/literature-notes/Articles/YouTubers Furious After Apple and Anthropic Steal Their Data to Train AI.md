- As [*Wired* reports](https://www.wired.com/story/youtube-training-data-apple-nvidia-anthropic/) with the help of the [data-driven *Proof News* project](https://www.proofnews.org/apple-nvidia-anthropic-used-thousands-of-swiped-youtube-videos-to-train-ai/), a dataset known as "YouTube Subtitles" has been used by everyone from Apple and Anthropic to Nvidia and Salesforce to train AI models since it was released in 2020.
